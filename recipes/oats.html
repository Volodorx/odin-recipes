Overnight Oats

An Easy Breakfast to Start the Day Right

Featuring crispy, golden-brown chicken paired with salty, savory bacon. Served over a lovely fresh salad, the combination of textures and bold flavors creates a hearty, satisfying dish that's both refreshing and indulgent.

Ingredients

  • Uncooked, Skin on Chicken Breast
  • 2 Slices of Smoked Bacon
  • Iceberg lettuce
  • Red Onion
  • Cucumber
  • Red Pepper
  • Cherry Tomatoes (optional)
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Mayonnaise
  • 1 Egg
  • Black Pepper

Method

  1. Set air fryer to roast setting at 180 celsius, for 25 minutes. When up to temperature, add chicken
  2. Chop up lettuce, cucmber, red pepper, onion and cherry tomates (if you are using them) and add to bowl
  3. Bring a pan of water to the boil and add egg (I put it in for 7 minutes for a jammy yolk but if you prefer it more set then boil for 9 minutes) When cooked put in a bowl of cold water to prevent overcooking
  4. Add a tablespoon of oil to a frying pan and fry bacon, set aside when cooked
  5. When chicken is cooked, shred into a bowl and add in the bacon (cut up), mayonnaise, grated cheddar cheese and a generous amount of black pepper. Mix well and add on top of salad
  6. Peel egg and cut into quarters, add on top of salad
