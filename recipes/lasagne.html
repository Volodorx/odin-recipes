Lasagne

A Hearty Italian Classic

Layers of rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and tender pasta sheets come together in this comforting Italian classic. Baked until golden and bubbling, this lasagne is the perfect hearty dish for family dinners or special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 750g lean beef mince
  • 90g prosciutto
  • 800g passata
  • 200ml beef stock
  • nutmeg
  • 300g fresh lasagne sheets
  • white sauce
  • 125g mozzarella

Method

  1. To make the meat sauce, heat 2 tbsp olive oil in a frying pan and cook 750g lean beef mince in two batches for about 10 mins until browned all over.
  2. Finely chop the prosciutto, then stir through the meat mixture.
  3. Pour over 800g passata and 200ml beef stock. Add a little grated nutmeg, then season with salt and pepper.
  4. Bring up to the boil, then simmer for 30 mins until the sauce looks rich.
  5. Heat the oven to 180C/160C fan/gas 4 and lightly oil an ovenproof dish (about 30 x 20cm).
  6. Spoon one third of the meat sauce into the dish, then cover with some fresh lasagne sheets. Drizzle over roughly 130g white sauce.
  7. Repeat until you have three layers of pasta. Cover with the remaining 390g white sauce, making sure you can’t see any pasta poking through.
  8. Scatter 125g torn mozzarella over the top.
  9. Arrange the rest of the prosciutto on top. Bake for 45 mins until the top is bubbling and lightly browned.
